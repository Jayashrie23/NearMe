MADURAI MEENAKSHI AMMAN TEMPLE

The Madurai Meenakshi Amman Temple is a historic and iconic Hindu temple located in the ancient city of Madurai, Tamil Nadu. It is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar, symbolizing divine love and harmony. The temple is famous for its towering gopurams, decorated with thousands of colorful sculptures. Its architectural marvels, such as the Thousand Pillar Hall and the Golden Lotus Tank, draw millions of visitors every year. Known as a cultural and spiritual landmark, the temple plays a central role in festivals like the Meenakshi Thirukalyanam.
